This foler has the files and data we use for Joint data imputation and product graph learning section of the paper.

AQI_Data.m -->
In this code, we apply the proposed (PGL) method for the task of joint
data imputation and product graph learning.

Here, we use the PM 2.5 data collected across 30 air quality monitoring
stations in India over the year 2018. The data is available at https://app.cpcbccr.com.

We, preprocess the data and save it in the usable format in the file AQI_Data_WithCoords.mat. 
Here, we also give the latitude and longitude loactions as the location coordinates for ploting the figures.
