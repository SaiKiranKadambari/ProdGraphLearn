This folder constains the files for learning the product graphs with missing entries.

We collect PM2.5 measurements in the air quality data collected across the 30 air quality monitoring stations located all over India for
the years 2018. The data is available at https://app.cpcbccr.com/

The data is stored in the usable format in the file AQI_Data_WithCoords.mat

The file AQI_Data.m gives the figure 5 of the paper.
